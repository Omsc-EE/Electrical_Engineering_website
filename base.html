OMSC Engineering

The College of Engineering which was originally came from the College of Architecture, Engineering and Technology (CAET) was established on 2013 which consist of Architecture, Industrial Engineering and Electrical Engineering, later on during Academic year 2019-2020 the Civil Engineering was added to the department, The College of Architecture, Engineering and Technology produces its first Electrical Engineering graduates on A.Y 2017-2018.

The College of Engineering was led by its dean, Engr Michelle Enriquez MSCE, MPA., and the Electrical Engineering program was headed by Engr. Abigail Gonzales MSEE.
